Project 3 Part B Home Page Conceptual Design Website Plan
Home Page Schedule Page Reservation Page Album Page About Page
This page will serve as a place for our website guests who are interested in visiting our resort but would like to do more research and get to know more about what we offer to see if we are the proper resort experience for them. On this page will be more information regarding the resort venue, our attractions, the rooms, packages offered, and anything people may be interested in learning more about. We will also offer a FAQ on this page for visitors to refer to. If there is something not on this page that the visitor still wants to know more about, we advise them to contact us using our email address or phone number and any notable questions will be answered and added to the FAQ. Links to other websites may also be included here with more information such as Florida or Panama centric tourism pages.

The contact portion of the page will feature several different emails for specific services that we offer. This will include but not be limited to a reservation email, guest folio request email, concierge email, spa email, restaurant email, and info email for general inquiries and guest questions. In addition to these emails will be our phone number and of course our address.

This page will feature images (.jpeg, .gif, .png), CSS for styling, hyperlinks to other pages, javascript, hyperlinks through images, and text breaks. This page will not feature video, radio buttons, html form, html table, flash, java applet, text formatting, or html frames.
