Packages

Several options are available for Spring Break visitors. Choose the package that fits the party you want to have!

Silver

This is the basic package available for visitors. This option includes complementary breakfast, vouchers for entrance to the in house club, and accommodations in one of our luxurious rooms.

Gold

This is the intermediate package available for visitors. This option includes complementary breakfast, unlimited access to the in house club, and accommodations in one of the party suites. 

Platinum

This is the premium package available for visitors. This option includes complementary food and drink from the hotel restaurant, unlimited access to the in house club's VIP section, and accommodations in one of the presidential suites.
