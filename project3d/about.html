Our Resort

Jade Palace is the club on the beach that you can sleep at! If you're looking for a place to kick it in the Panama City Area, Jade Palace is the place to be. Aimed to satisfy party goers all over the world, we provide an electric atmosphere while also giving guests a relaxing home to reside within when the partying is in intermission. At Jade Palace guests can expect the time of their lives as this resort is one you'll never forget.

Services

As an alternative to the usual resort accomodations, Jade Palace provides a variety of features including unique ones that can not be found at any other resort. We strive to create a home for our guests along with the facilities to be able to party as desired. Services include:

  • 24 hour guest assistance and room service
  • Packages include: Romantic, Adventure, and Group
  • Indoor and Outdoor Pools (with jacuzzis)
  • Outdoor Party Area along the beach
  • Indoor Party Ballrooms with beach view
  • Express Laundry
  • Fully Equipped Fitness Center
  • World-renowned restaurant and chefs (includes buffet)
  • Resort Boutiques for Shopping
